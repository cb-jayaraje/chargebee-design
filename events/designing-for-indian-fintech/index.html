Designing for FinTech:
The Indian Journey

Our first talk centres around the Challenges in Designing for Finance
and Technology and the Future of FinTech in India.

With: Dharmesh, Design Lead | Setu
When: September 21st 2019, 11 AM to 1 PM
Where: Chargebee HO, Chennai

How do we create FinTech applications that India’s rural and semi-urban population can use? Is it possible to make these applications affordable?

These are some of the many questions that Setu and D91 Labs are trying to answer with their work. Our speaker Dharmesh, who leads design at Setu, will break down how inclusive research and design in FinTech will simplify journeys for the next billion tech adopters in India.

Understanding the Indian user is essential to offering them the most relevant experiences, and is something Dharmesh and his team have been exploring both in their work and in their article series ‘Decoding Bharat’.

As of June 2019,
UPI has clocked transactions worth
₹1.46 trillion, growing 3X since 2018.

We will also talk about

- The role of mobile first platforms like UPI in revolutionising the way we interact with FinTech.

- How KYC and UPI experiences are on the road to becoming more seamless

- The burgeoning space of ‘account aggregators’ who will do for the secure management of your accounts what Uber did for ride-sharing.

As we interact with more digital payments and solutions that depend on these platforms everyday, a nuanced view of this exciting space is essential. If you are a designer, startup founder or a FinTech enthusiast, we would be delighted to have you join us.

About Our Speaker

Dharmesh is the design lead at Setu, a company working to overhaul India’s FinTech architecture to be more inclusive and affordable. He is an alumnus of NID Ahmedabad, previously worked for Cleartax, and is passionate about all things FinTech.
